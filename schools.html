Frequently Asked Questions

Questions schools often ask

Here are some common questions I receive before a school project begins.

Can I see a mockup before printing?

Yes. For projects that benefit from a mockup, I'll prepare one before production so we can confirm the details together.

Do you accept rush orders?

Whenever possible, yes. It depends on my current workload and the scope of the project. I'd rather be honest than promise something I can't deliver well.

Can you deliver outside La Union?

Yes. I've completed projects for clients in different provinces across the Philippines.

What information do you need before printing?

Usually the design, quantity, sizes (if applicable), and any logos or school branding needed for the project.
